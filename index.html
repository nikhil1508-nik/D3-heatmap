This website presents every movie, tv or celebrity mention on every episode of Community TV series by every main character.

"Community makes heavy use of meta-humor and pop culture references, often parodying film and television clich√©s and tropes" — Wikipedia

We wanted to prove that, quantifiably and visually.

There are some limitations though. In order to keep the graphs readable and scannable, we included only the seven starring actors. Also, the real number of pop references is higher, since books and music didn't make the cut.

The heatmaps are built with D3.js. The typeface is Karla. The color palette is based on ColorBrewer. Content data courtesy of a fan wiki and IMDB.
